Findora Wallet Application - Instructions for Installing the Zipped Folder

1. Download the Findora Wallet Application zipped folder from https://developer.findora.org/.

2. Unzip the extension.zip folder. 

3. Open Google Chrome.

4. Click the "three-dot" button at the upper right corner of Google Chrome.

5. Select "More Tools" -> "Extenstions".

6. Click "Load unpacked".

7. Select the unzipped "extensions" folder.

8. Click the "Extensions" icon at the upper right corner of Google Chrome.

9. Pin the "Findora Wallet Application" extension.

10. Click the purple Findora icon and play around with the Findora Wallet Application!
